    I’m running because I’ve seen how powerful and passionate the Republican youth can be when they are engaged.
    I believe if we provide the opportunity for them to be involved in meaningful ways, we can Grow Our Party and better our state.

ABOUT ME

I was adopted when I was two years old while my mom and brother were on a mission trip and realized that God was calling them to bring me into their family. I grew up in Farmington Hills but also lived in the Grand Rapids area for a few years. I’ve been blessed to attend a variety of schools- Christian, public, Spanish immersion, and early college. I feel that the different environments have given me a unique perspective on many aspects of life.

I currently attend Oakland Early College where I take both high school and college classes, and am on track to earn an Associate’s Degree upon graduation. I plan to stay in Michigan for my four-year major in Political Science, with a minor in Business/Marketing/Economics.

In the past year I’ve been honored to be a part of Student Statesmanship Institute, Michigan Excellence in Public Service Series, the Schuette for Governor campaign, and the Michigan Republican Party’s Election Day Operations program.

I’m passionate about helping others and being a part of the change that I want to see in the world, which is why I’ve gone on multiple international mission trips, volunteered with children who have special needs, and pushed to engage other youth in politics.
